## Overview

This text explores the metaphysical origins and technological legacy of a **non-corporeal race** that eventually became the legendary inhabitants of **Atlantis**. The author posits that these beings were originally **pure mindstreams** who became trapped in physical forms through an addiction to sensory experience, leading to a spiritual descent and the eventual **division of sexes**. The narrative serves to validate the psychic work of **Edgar Cayce** while warning modern humanity that this ancient, stored knowledge is about to be unearthed to trigger a **global resurrection of consciousness**.

## **The Great Descent: _The Evolutionary Journey of the Atlantean Mindstream_**

### 1. Foundations: The Non-Corporeal Origin

In the primordial era of our planet’s history, the entities we categorize as "Atlantean" existed in an ontological state entirely removed from the biological imperatives of the modern human. They were not bipeds, nor were they bound by the constraints of a singular physical locus; they were noncorporeal mindstreams—vast, sentient energetic envelopes that navigated the terrestrial sphere as "aliens" to the material experience. These beings did not occupy space so much as they projected an "atmosphere" that moved through the environment, untouched by the gravity of bone and sinew.

**Primary Characteristics of the Original Mindstream:**

- **Energetic Fluidity:** They lacked any fixed biomorphic structure, existing as self-contained "atmospheres" of consciousness rather than discrete physical organisms.
- **A-Temporal Continuity:** Their original state was not limited by linear time; they perceived reality as a totalized flow rather than a sequence of events.
- **Pre-Linguistic Resonance:** Their primary frequency was rooted in the sound **"atl."** This is the oldest meaningful resonance on Earth, surviving through millennia as a vibrational echo of their energetic origin—a period before the "weight" of matter silenced direct mental actuality.

Evidence of this ancient presence remains etched into the biological world. The incorruptible biological memories of eels, which navigate across the vastness of the Atlantic to the Sargasso Sea, serve as a lingering anthropological marker of the spawning estuaries that once lined the shores of a vanished world. This transition from pure energy to biological memory began with a profound curiosity regarding the tactile furnace of life.

### 2. The Lure of Matter: The Mechanics of Interaction

The descent of these mindstreams was not a sudden fall, but a gradual, seductive entrapment. Their interaction with the material plane is best understood through the analogy of a "cold swimming pool" on a sweltering day: the initial immersion is a shocking, sensory-rich thrill that is "great once you get in." This sensory appeal precipitated a systematic exploration that moved through three distinct stages of temporal embedment:

1. **Species Projection:** Initially, the Atlanteans did not inhabit individual bodies. They projected their awareness into entire "continuities"—experiencing the collective life of herds and flocks rather than discrete objects.
2. **Rhythms of Process:** In this stage, they did not perceive "things," but rather the flow of growth, the pulse of mitochondria, and the shifting interlinked life of the planet’s biosphere.
3. **Individualized Consciousness:** Eventually, the fascination with the "spark" of life led to the establishment of "points of focus." They narrowed their multi-dimensional consciousness into singular, individualized forms.

The mindstreams viewed this narrowing as significant technological progress. To them, the ability to focus on a singular "point" in space was a novel achievement. In their profound denial, they failed to recognize that this focus was the first stage of metaphysical atrophy, leading them away from the holistic understanding of the "One" toward the fragmenting trap of individual survival.

### 3. Biological Engineering and the "Superior" Pattern

To stabilize their presence on the material plane, the Atlanteans selected a specific "anthropoid" base as a vessel. They did not allow this species to follow a natural evolutionary trajectory; instead, they "in-formed" the biological matter with high-order energetic patterns, disrupting the natural timeline to create a form they deemed superior. These created beings were essentially the bio-mechanical slaves of the mindstreams—the ancestors of the modern "freed slave" that is humanity.

### 4. The Addiction: Sensuality and the Great Separation

The experience of inhabiting a biological "furnace of life" became a profound addiction. This obsession with the sensory world led to a catastrophic cleavage of their original nature, marking the transition from being to having.

> "When it is said that the division of the sexes came only recently, the cleavage of what had been one androgynous mindstream became two—male and female consciousness."

As the androgynous mindstream fractured into binary sexes, the Atlanteans lost the "mind-to-mind actuality" of their origins. They became mired in the biological functions they once merely observed. Spoken and written languages emerged as "secondary images"—crude compensations for the loss of telepathic teleology. This caused a divergence into two philosophical paths:

- **Children of One:** Those who sought to maintain a connection to the divine source, viewing the disruption of the planet as a violation of natural law.
- **Children of Belial (The Darkbound):** Those who focused entirely on the survival of the physiological creature, believing no divinity existed beyond their own material presence.

### 5. Technological Compensation: The Living Crystals

As their internal psi energies decayed, the Atlanteans turned to external machinery. They sought to project their failing concentration into solid objects, substituting "toys" for mental effort. The pinnacle of this externalization was the creation of "Living Crystals."

**Technical Specification: Atlantean Living Crystals**

- **Composition:** Exceedingly pure silicon infused with trace metallic elements.
- **Function:** They were to Atlantean consciousness what the transistor is to modern electronics—hard drives capable of being "impressed" with the total contents of a mindstream.
- **Power Source:** Direct interaction with the Earth’s electromagnetic field, maintaining a vibrant, indestructible resonance.
- **Externalized Divinity:** These crystals were placed in temples as "sacred objects." They held the memories of the "Origin Time," becoming something the Atlanteans had to look at externally because they could no longer find it within.

The tragedy of the later Atlantean period was a state of profound denial. They believed their technology could solve the problem of death—a concept they had only recently discovered—but their devices only served to embed them further into the "solid state" of matter.

### 6. Consequences: The Fractured Earth and the Hidden Legacy

Approximately 80,000 years in the past, the overuse of high-energy technology to maintain their failing corporeality reached a tipping point. The massive energy draw created a **"magnetic vacuole"** beneath the continent, causing a literal fracturing of the Earth’s crust.

- **Fragmented Perception:** The Atlanteans became stranded in the five physical senses, losing the ability to perceive the "interlinked life of the planet."
- **The Hall of Records:** Realizing the end was near, they buried "Living Crystals" in repositories, most notably the Chamber of Records near the Sphinx on the Giza plateau.

The hidden chamber near the Sphinx contains a primary crystal impressed with the total history of their culture. Its discovery will not require the study of an ancient language; rather, the presence of human minds will trigger a "radio broadcast." The information will be projected via direct perception, bypassing the clunky mechanics of speech and providing a direct understanding of the Great Pyramid’s purpose and the "terrific sadness" of the Atlantean failure.

**Final Learning Narrative Statement** The history of Atlantis is a warning implicit: a chronicle of how a "Golden Age" was lost when divinity was externalized into technology. However, there is a promise of resurrection. The remnants of these ancient mindstreams still reside within the modern human genetic code. By recognizing this buried heritage, we may finally reconcile the divide between the physical and the divine, allowing for a restoration that is not merely metaphorical, but one that manifests "even in the flesh."
