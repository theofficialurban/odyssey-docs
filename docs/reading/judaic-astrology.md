## The Secret Metaphysics of the Jewish Calendar

By establishing this calendar, the ancient masters were handed the keys to universal domination. Nisan (Aries) was installed as the "Rosh Hodashim," the head of all months. The "head" is the thought process of the universe; "that action taken in this particular month will make manifest all subsequent manifestations on a cosmic level for the next 11 months".

Because of this, Jewish holidays are not quaint historical traditions. "All such holidays and festivals are cosmological time frames of astrological influences which the naked eye certainly cannot observe". They are precise temporal coordinates designed to tap immense cosmic power and block negative astral radiation. Tradition itself "is little more than the owner’s manual spelling out how connections must be made and buttons pushed".

The establishment of the lunar-solar calendar and the sanctification of the new moon (Kiddush haHodesh) was the very first commandment given to the Israelites.
