# Aristotle ↔ Bible Glossary (Extended Edition) {#title}

This document maps core Aristotelian philosophical terms to biblical concepts, focusing on the formation of the soul, faith, works, wisdom, and fulfillment. It is designed for theological, philosophical, and comparative study.

## Core Framework

:::highlight
`Dynamis` → `Hexis` → `Energeia` / `Entelecheia Potential` → `Disposition` / `Habit` → `Actualization` / `Fulfillment Biblical parallels: Faith` → `Works` → `Fruit; Seed` → `Cultivation` → `Harvest`
:::

## Tables

:::tabs
== Soul & Potential

| Term      | Definition                            | Biblical Parallel                       |
| --------- | ------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| `Dynamis` | Potentiality, capacity to become      | Faith, the seed of the Word (Luke 8:11) |
| `Nous`    | Intellective faculty of understanding | Spiritual insight and wisdom (Prov 4:7) |
| `Physis`  | Inner nature, principle of growth     | Created nature, Imago Dei (Gen 1:27)    |
| `Kinesis` | Change, movement toward actuality     | Transformation in Christ (2 Cor 3:18)   |
| `Arche`   | First principle or origin             | God as Creator (Gen 1:1)                |

== Habits & Works

| Term         | Definition                                   | Biblical Parallel                            |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| `Hexis`      | Stable disposition formed by repeated action | Works and formed character (James 2:17)      |
| `Ethos`      | Character shaped by habit                    | Integrity and witness (Prov 10:9)            |
| `Praxis`     | Action expressing character                  | Living out faith (James 1:22)                |
| `Enkrateia`  | Self-mastery                                 | Self-control, fruit of the Spirit (Gal 5:23) |
| `Sophrosyne` | Temperance and moderation                    | Sobriety and discipline (Titus 2:12)         |

== Actualization & Fruit

| Term          | Definition                                 | Biblical Parallel                          |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| `Energeia`    | Being-at-work, realized activity           | Faith working through love (Gal 5:6)       |
| `Entelecheia` | Complete actuality, fulfillment of purpose | Bearing lasting fruit (John 15:8)          |
| `Arete`       | Excellence or virtue                       | Holiness and righteousness (1 Pet 1:15–16) |
| `Teleios`     | Complete or mature                         | Maturity in Christ (Phil 3:12–14)          |
| `Eudaimonia`  | Human flourishing                          | Blessed life (Matt 5:3–12)                 |

== Wisdom & Truth

| Term           | Definition             | Biblical Parallel                       |
| -------------- | ---------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| `Sophia`       | Contemplative wisdom   | Knowledge of God (Prov 2:6)             |
| `Phronesis`    | Practical wisdom       | Wise obedience (Matt 7:24)              |
| `Episteme`     | Demonstrable knowledge | Knowing God through creation (Rom 1:20) |
| `Aletheia`     | Truth, unconcealment   | God’s Word as truth (John 17:17)        |
| `Orthos Logos` | Right reasoning        | Sound doctrine (2 Tim 4:3)              |

== Purification & Correction

| Term           | Definition                             | Biblical Parallel                 |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| `Katharsis`    | Purification through removal of excess | Repentance and renewal (Ps 51:10) |
| `Epanorthosis` | Moral correction or restoration        | Turning back to God (Acts 3:19)   |
| `Aischyne`     | Proper shame regulating conduct        | Humility before God (Prov 22:4)   |

== Order, Purpose & Community

| Term         | Definition                       | Biblical Parallel                    |
| ------------ | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| `Telos`      | Final cause or purpose           | God’s calling and destiny (Rom 8:28) |
| `Sustasis`   | Ordered structure or arrangement | Body of Christ (1 Cor 12)            |
| `Sympatheia` | Harmony among parts              | Unity in love (Rom 12:10)            |
| `Koinonia`   | Shared life and participation    | Fellowship in Christ (Acts 2:42)     |
| `Logos`      | Rational ordering principle      | The Word (John 1:1)                  |

:::
